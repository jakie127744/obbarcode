Scanning Issues

If your barcodes aren''t scanning, check the following:

  • Quiet Zone: Ensure there is enough white space around the barcode.
  • Contrast: Always print black bars on a white background.
  • Scale: If the barcode is too small, it may exceed the DPI limits of your scanner. Use the "Scale" setting to increase width.

Batch CSV Printing

For batch generation, ensure your CSV follows the header format: format,data (e.g., QR,https://molave.io). Use the provided "Template" button in the Batch section for a sample file.
